Subtitle Player - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

Subtitle Player is a subtitle reader for your phone. This policy covers both the website (spencerlee.net/subtitle-player) and the Android app.

The short version

Subtitle Player does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything happens on your device. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party services.

Subtitle files

When you open a subtitle file (.srt or .vtt), it is read and displayed entirely on your device. The file and its contents are never uploaded, sent over the network, or stored by us. Nothing leaves your device.

Data we collect

None. The app has no servers and makes no network requests for its core function. It does not use cookies for tracking. A single optional preference (whether to show upcoming subtitle lines) may be saved locally in your browser or app storage; it never leaves your device.

Permissions

The app only accesses a subtitle file that you explicitly choose to open. It requests no other device permissions and needs no network access.

Children

Subtitle Player does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "last updated" date.
