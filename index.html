How to use

1. Load an .srt or .vtt subtitle file.
2. Press Play the moment the movie starts
   on your TV.
3. Tap the subtitle area to play or pause.
4. Jump 10s with the rewind/forward buttons,
   or drag the scrubber to seek anywhere.
5. If the subtitles drift out of sync, nudge
   them with the -0.5 / +0.5 buttons; reset
   sets the offset back to zero.
6. The screen stays awake while playing, and
   the fullscreen button hides everything else.

Everything runs in your browser. Nothing is
uploaded anywhere.
